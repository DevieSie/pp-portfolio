Couple words about me

Hi! I'm Piotr Purta, a software developer from Poland with a dream for creating things — especially games. By day, I work with C# and Angular, building applications that solve real problems. But my true passion lies in game development, fueled by a lifelong love for fantasy books and computer games. From epic RPGs to strategy masterpieces, gaming has been my inspiration since childhood.


Right now, though, my biggest project is my family. I'm a proud dad to three wonderful daughters, and every day is an adventure of parenting, supporting my wife, and tackling home projects. We're in the middle of building our own house and preparing for a major life change — moving from a city apartment to the countryside. It's the biggest uncertainty on the horizon, but also the most exciting chapter yet.


When I'm not coding or chasing kids around, you'll find me deep in a fantasy novel or sketching game ideas. This site is where I share my journey: my CV, programming projects, game dev experiments, photography, and that infamous "Pile of Shame" backlog of unfinished games. Whether you're here for tech, games, or just curious about balancing family life with coding dreams — welcome!
